Creamy Mushroom Soup

This is a creamy mushroom soup to warm you up on a cold day, pairs well with tempeh wrap and fries (blender or food processor required)

  • Shiitake mushrooms
  • Minced shallots
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Tarragon
  • Heavy cream
  • Vegetable stock
  1. Heat olive oil on medium high heat, once hot swirl in butter, once butter has melted add mushrooms
  2. Remove 1 cup of mushrooms and set aside
  3. Add shallots and garlic to pot, cook for about a minute
  4. Add salt, tarragon and stock. Increase the heat and cook uncovered for 10 minutes. Remove from heat.
  5. Add mixture to blender and puree until smooth. Then return to pot
  6. Stir in cream and reserved mushrooms, adjust salt and pepper to taste
