Tempeh Wrap

This Temeph wrap is a healthy and delicious lunch alternative to any meat based wrap. It has the perfect balance of flavors.

  • Tempeh
  • Spinach
  • Avocado
  • Tahini
  • Cranberry Sauce
  • Orange juice and zest
  • Tortillas
  • lemon juice
  • Tamari
  1. Add tahini, lemon juice and water. Whisk until smooth and creamy
  2. Zest and juice orange and add to cranberry sauce. Mix well
  3. Heat pan and add Crumbled tempeh and tamari
  4. Cook until tempeh is browned and crispy on the edges
  5. Heat tortilla and spead orange cranberry sauce and tahini
  6. Layer spinach, avocado, tempeh and fold in sides then tightly roll away from you
  7. Enjoy!
