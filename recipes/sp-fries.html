Sweet Potato Fries

This is a quick and easy recipe for sweet potato fries. An air fryer is required

  • Sweet potato
  • Canola oil
  • Garlic powder
  • Pepper
  • Ground sweet paprika
  1. Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  2. Cut sweet potato into 1/2-inch wide fries. Place in a bowl with canola oil; toss to coat. Season with spices; Mix until evenly coated.
  3. Place an even layer of fries in air fryer basket; working in batches if necessary.
  4. Cook until golden, around 10 minutes. Repeat to cook remaining fries
  5. Serve hot; Enjoy!
